About Us

Phantom Records is a Maryland-based online record store. We specialize in making custom mix tapes from your existing music collection. We also have ten pre-made mixes available from our store. About Mix Tapes

Traditional mix tapes, also called music compilations, are collections of songs placed one after the other; they have short silences between each. DJ-mixed tapes, also known as non-stop mixes, blend from one song to the next, creating an uninterrupted listening experience. This requires beat matching skill and incorporates timed transitions between songs. Click Mix Tapes to read more.
