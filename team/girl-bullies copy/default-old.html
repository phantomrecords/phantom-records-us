DJs and Executives 

Over the years, mix tape DJs and music industry executives have had an interesting relationship. DJs create exciting transitions between songs while breaking new tracks after finding interesting places to fit them into a mix, and executives let it happen so music listening crowds can respond to or reject new songs.
