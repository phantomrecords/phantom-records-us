Know Why I Do What I Do — My History

Phantom Records, the DBA, was first registered 1989 in the East Bay Area of California. It was created as a small business after the proprietor held a few parties during his high-school years that were profitable. His penchant for marketing drew the attention of his parents and after graduating high school he said he wanted to go into the work as a job one day. After months of family meetings, his mother gave him the money to register Phantom Records. He was not given the choice of attending school because his family couldn’t afford to send him while his other two brothers were in school. So this was his compromise.

Learning about the music business kept him busy and eventually led to decades of research until his mother gave him the money to upgrade his business into a partnership after he proved he could run a successful startup when he broke even running an online store overseas with only $2,000 of investment. He ran his business overseas for two years and moved it to the United States when he returned, opening a partnership with his mother as a silent investor. Together they have continued as partners while he studied the industry to choose a path forward.

Now, in 2025, after the final tier two of the Copyright Royalty Board’s streaming rate (a tax-like amount all streams are charged when played online) became final — along with all related Federal and local guidelines — he launched his Partnership’s website with all the proper rates set to current levels, making it the only website in the U.S. (and hence the world) that follows every law and copyright guideline on the books.

To see what he sells, visit https://dj-experience.app — you’ll see something you’ve never seen before: a DJ-mix website that sells and streams digital mixtapes online without allowing anything to be downloaded or purchased in any physical or data format. The site does not allow a buyer to take anything away, which fulfills the essential goal of U.S. music laws. His model is the only one that fully supports this goal and has won him several awards listed below.
