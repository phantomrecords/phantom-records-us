Mix Tapes (or Mix Disc Compilations)

Mix Tapes and Mix Discs refer to the same product. Private mixes (not to be confused with public mixes) are typically made up of copyrighted pop music taken from a listener's collection. Mixtapes and Mix Discs transition from song to song creating an uninterrupted listening experience. They are perfect for private social events or background music during private parties, outdoor gatherings, exercise routines, driving, or relaxing after work.

Compilations

Compilations are collections of songs usually put together by a recording company or a listener based on a particular mood or purpose. For example, they are popular as Wedding keepsakes. Compilations, by convention, are organized by genre, time period, tempo or social setting. Although compilations are popular, we believe non-stop mixes, with uninterrupted and timed transitions between songs, are meaningful additions to any listener's collection.
