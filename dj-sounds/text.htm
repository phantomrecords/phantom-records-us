Mix Tapes

A mix tape (commonly the two words are stuck together as mixtape), or mixed tape, is a homemade compilation of songs (typically copyrighted pop music taken from another source) recorded in a specific order. It is generally used at private locations as a soundtrack to social events, working out, driving, or dates. 

Compilations

A compilation is a collection of songs put together by a recording company for sale at retail. They bring less attention to the person who chose the songs (the compiler) and more to the genre or time period of the music included. Although their popularity is not in debate, nothing meets the needs of a music listener better than a tape they make on their own.
