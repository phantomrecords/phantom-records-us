Michael Widener
General Partner and Web Programmer

Michael Widener is general partner and web programmer at Phantom Records. He is currently attending American University full-time to complete his undergrad. He's studying Multimedia Design and Development and Computer Science. He's on course to graduate in 2007 and is piloting the company's product line to investigate its promise.

Stephen Widener 
Product Demographer

Stephen Widener is Michael's twin brother. He gives the company essential pointers on which songs should be incorporated into the final product. He graduated from the University of San Francisco in Economics. When not putting in time for the company, Stephen Widener works for the Bureau of Labor Statistics. His attention to product pricing in his work on the consumer price index gives him a firm understanding of how much consumers spend on similar products so we can price our products accordingly.
